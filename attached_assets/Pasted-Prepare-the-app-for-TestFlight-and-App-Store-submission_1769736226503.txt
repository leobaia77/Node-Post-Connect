Prepare the app for TestFlight and App Store submission.

REQUIRED ITEMS:

1. Privacy Policy Screen:
   - Accessible from Settings and onboarding
   - Clear explanation of data collected
   - How data is used (personalization only)
   - EXPLICIT statement: "We never use your health data for advertising"
   - How to delete your data
   - Contact information

2. Terms of Service Screen:
   - Age requirement (13+)
   - Not medical advice disclaimer
   - Parent/guardian responsibility
   - Acceptable use

3. Data Export Feature (Settings):
   - "Export My Data" button
   - Generates JSON/CSV of all user data
   - Email or download option

4. Account Deletion:
   - "Delete My Account" in Settings
   - Confirmation flow
   - Immediate deletion + 30-day backup
   - Email confirmation

5. Offline Support:
   - Cache last 7 days of data
   - Allow logging when offline
   - Sync queue for when connection restored
   - Clear offline indicator

6. Error Handling:
   - User-friendly error messages
   - Retry mechanisms
   - "Contact Support" option for persistent errors
   - Crash reporting (Sentry or similar)

7. Accessibility:
   - VoiceOver labels on all interactive elements
   - Dynamic type support
   - Sufficient color contrast
   - Reduce motion option

8. Loading States:
   - Skeleton screens
   - Pull-to-refresh everywhere applicable
   - Progress indicators for long operations

9. Empty States:
   - Helpful prompts when no data
   - "Get Started" actions
   - Illustrations

10. App Icon and Splash:
    - Professional app icon (not health-related symbols that could mislead)
    - Splash screen with app name

APP STORE METADATA TO PREPARE:
- App name: [Your chosen name]
- Subtitle: "Evidence-based guidance for active teens"
- Description: Focus on education, support, NOT medical treatment
- Keywords: teen health, sports, sleep, recovery, parent monitoring
- Screenshots: Show teen and parent experiences
- Age rating: 12+ (no medical treatment claims)
- Privacy nutrition labels: Accurate based on data collected

TESTFLIGHT PREP:
- Configure TestFlight in App Store Connect
- Internal testing group first
- Then limited external beta
- Feedback collection mechanism

Remember: The app provides educational guidance, not medical diagnosis or treatment. All messaging must be consistent with this positioning.
